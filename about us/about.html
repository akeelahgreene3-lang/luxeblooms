Our Story

Founded by two sisters with a love for gardening, Luxe Blooms began as a small passion project rooted in a deep appreciation for nature’s beauty. What started as growing flowers in our own backyard soon blossomed into something much more meaningful.

During the Covid-19 pandemic, with not much else to do, we found joy in the beauty of a floral touch and the creation of pieces that could brighten someone’s day or add a special touch to life’s most important moment. Whether it was a bouquet for a birthday, a centrepiece for a family gathering, or flowers offered in times of comfort, each arrangement was made with intention and heart.

What started as home sales quickly grew as friends, family, and the local community began to notice the elegance and care in every design. In 2018, Luxe Blooms reached a major milestone by opening its very first store, allowing the sisters to share our floral creations with an even wider audience. The business continued to thrive, and three additional branches were opened in the following years, bringing Luxe Blooms’ handcrafted arrangements to more communities across Trinidad.

Despite its growth, Luxe Blooms has remained true to its roots. Every bouquet, wreath, or centrepiece is still created with the same passion, care, and personal touch that defined its humble beginnings.

Today, Luxe Blooms continues to celebrate life’s moments, whether big or small, through flowers that tell a story, evoke emotion, and leave a lasting impression.
